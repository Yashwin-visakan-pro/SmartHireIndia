SmartHire India

Using AI to Close the Employment Gap

Project Description

India suffers from an enormous employment paradox: while millions of qualified and trained youths are unemployed or underemployed, employers, SMEs, startups, and solo-hirers struggle to find suitable candidates affordably and on time.

The disconnection is most severe in rural and semiurban regions, where access to job listings, career guidance, and efficient hiring tools remains limited.

Key Challenges

  • Insufficient job transparency for rural job seekers
  • Mismatch between qualifications and market skills
  • Outdated recruitment platforms and processes
  • Lack of personalized career counseling
  • Limited access for mobile and regional language users
